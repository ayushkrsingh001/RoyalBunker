About Royal Bunkers Reloaded

Where Gaming Meets Premium Living

Royal Bunkers Reloaded is a premium in-house gaming arena designed for students and residents of Royal Castle Hostel & PG. Built with high-performance gaming setups, professional pool tables, PlayStation consoles, and a comfortable lounge atmosphere, it offers the perfect space for entertainment, tournaments, and late-night matches.

Royal Castle Hostel & PG is a modern residential facility providing secure, comfortable, and student-friendly accommodation. With quality living spaces, essential amenities, and a vibrant community environment, the hostel ensures both comfort and lifestyle balance.

Royal Bunkers Reloaded is proudly operated under Royal Castle Hostel & PG by Sadguru Corporation, committed to delivering premium facilities, innovation-driven services, and a high-quality living experience.
